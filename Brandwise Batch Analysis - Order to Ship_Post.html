Objective

  • Sample size = 156 observations
  • Number of observations excluded from batch data = 53

An interesting note is that it takes about as long on average to post an invoice as it does to ship an order.

Conclusion

Based on a sample of 156 Brandwise batches received between 1/9 - 1/19, orders take approximately 7.8 days on average from order entry in Brandwise to ship. They take an additional 8.2 days on average to post. The time from order creation to posted invoice takes an average of 16 days. I was unable to identify a noticeable pattern based on order size.

Recomendations

  1. Examine the process from shipment to posted invoice and find ways to reduce this interval.

  2. Try to identify the underlying cause for the orders that took longer than 15 days to ship.
